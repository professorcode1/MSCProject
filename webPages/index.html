Rules

  • The event begins on IST 00:00 27th Febraury and ends on IST 12:00 28th February
  • Anyone above the age of 13 can participate in the event.
  • Individual as well as team registrations with maximum 3 members are allowed.
  • Team Member(s) can be of different Institutes/Schools.
  • Registration will be closed 24 hours before the event begins.

Event Format

  • Event consists of 2 stages
    • Stage 1: You have to collect all the relics from each section by following the clues.
    Stage 2: This stage will unclock only if you have solved all the questions from each section. This will be the final stage of event.Team completing this stage first will win the competition

Input Format

  • Only alphanumeric and lowercase characters are allowed (no special characters are allowed).
  • No Spaces are allowed.
  • Final answer should be a string (with no spaces) even if your answer is of multiple words.
  • For e.g. If your answer is Microsoft Learn Chapter 2020, Then the correct form of the answer should be microsoftlearnchapter2020.

Scoring

  • For each correct answer 100 points will be rewarded. There will be no negative marking and you can answer as many times.
  • For each question, you'll be provided with 3 hints which would penalize you with 10, 20, 30 points respectively.
  • For stage 2 common hints will be posted on Discord community of MLSCDB.
  • Anyone if found guilty of cheating in any form will be disqualified.
  • Decision of judges will be final and the winner of the event will be announced on Instagram.
